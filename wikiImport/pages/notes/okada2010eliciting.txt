h1. Notes

Knowledge cartography
  * concept mapping - concepts they know and don't know
  * mapmaking - scaffolds different forms of reasoning about argument
  * critical thinking (Jonassen)

For PhD students
  * research map for designing a research project
  * reference map for collecting references in the literature
  * reading map for selecting key ideas of papers' content
  * theory map for organizing key concepts and definitions from the literature
  * fieldwork map for structuring key data from a corpus of documents
  * writing map for integrating key arguments for an essay

**inquiry maps** - graphical representations of knowledge during research process. help identify, connect and interpret key issues, ideas, concepts, data, arguments
