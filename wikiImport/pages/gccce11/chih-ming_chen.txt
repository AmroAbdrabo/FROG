h2. Digital library with reading annotation tool for supporting effective reading learning

Web-based reading interface where users can share annotations. 

Taiwan Digital History Library.

**Most libraries ignore the importance of archiving readers' reading knowledge**

Annotations can help the content of digital libraries dynamically grow with readers' knowledge contribution.

Annotated material helps "reading performance promotion" by providing more useful and fruitful knowledge.

Annotated info from different readers can help us explore value-added reading knowledge utilizing data-mining techniques.
