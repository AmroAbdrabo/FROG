h2. Related to CSCL-intro
  * Gerry Stahl - Drexel - Math Forum: http://gerrystahl.net/teaching/index.html (several CSCW and HCI courses, but also some older CSCL courses)
  * Dan Suthers - UHawaii - representational guidance - http://lilt.ics.hawaii.edu/suthers/ (links to some courses in Discourse, I have a login - courses on HCI and collective intelligence)
  * Carolyn Rose Penstein - CMU - machine learning and analytics - http://www.cs.cmu.edu/~cprose/Teaching.html - one on machine learning, and one on CSCL
  * Ravi Vatrapu - Copenhagen Business School (did PhD under Dan Suthers) - http://www.cs.cmu.edu/~cprose/Teaching.html - courses on technology enhanced teaching
  * Erik Duval - Leuven - hypermedia and databases -  has course on HCI http://erikduval.wordpress.com/about/
  * Sadhana Puntambekar - U Wisconsin-Madison - two courses: Interactive Learning Environment, and Cognition of Digital Information. http://www.compassproject.net/sadhana/index.htm
  * Pierre Dillenbourg - Polytechnic Lausanne - course on Computer Supported Collaborative Work - http://infowww.epfl.ch/imoniteur_ISAP/!itffichecours.htm?ww_i_matiere=1770948&ww_x_anneeAcad=39486325&ww_i_section=249847&ww_i_niveau=6683147&ww_c_langue=en
  * Caroline Haythornthwaite - UBC - courses on distributed knowledge and community informatics - http://haythorn.wordpress.com/teaching/
  * Heidy Maldonado - PhD student at Stanford - some older courses on HCI, online learning communities, and visualizations in teaching - http://www.stanford.edu/~kiky/teaching.html
  * Chip Bruce - iSchool at Illinois - inquiry based learning plus courses with Haythornthwaite - http://chipbruce.wordpress.com/teaching/
  * Amy Bruckman - College of Computing Georgia Tech - lot's of courses of design of online communities etc - http://www.cc.gatech.edu/~asb/teaching/index.html
  * Paul Dourish - UC Irvine - Lot's of courses on ubiquitous computing, research methods etc http://www.dourish.com/teaching.html
  * Alberto Barbosa Raposo - U Pontefico Rio de Janeiro - course on HCI in Portuguese - http://www.tecgraf.puc-rio.br/~abraposo/
  * Jörg Haake - Distance university in Hagen - courses in CSCW and CSCL in German - http://www.pi6.fernuni-hagen.de/Lehre/
  * Gerhard Fischer - UC Boulder - life-long learning - courses on Design, Creativity and New Media http://l3d.cs.colorado.edu/~gerhard/courses/index.html
  * Anders Mørch, UiO, intro to collaborative learning with technology http://www.uio.no/studier/emner/matnat/ifi/TOOL5100/h09/undervisningsplan.xml
